Swach Bharat, Swach Goan



Mission

To contribute towards the most popular and essential campaign started by our honorable prime minister Mr. Narendra Modi -Swach Bharat Abhiyan. Our campaign looks forward to the elimination of open defecation, conversion of unsanitary toilets to pour flush toilets, eradication of manual scavenging, municipal solid waste management, and spreading awareness and bringing about a behavioral change in people regarding waste management and healthy sanitation practices.

The Problem

Improper waste management is one of the main reasons for our polluted river and lakes. Wastes are thrown here and there on road, the toilets don’t have flush and in villages even today don’t have toilets.

Solution

The first step towards the solution of this issue is to spread awareness among people regarding this. Our door to door campaign is aimed at spreading awareness and we are also conducting a survey of the toilet in the village houses. We aim to make toilets in the villages, which will stop the issue of open defecation. Installation of public dustbins for proper waste dumping management.
